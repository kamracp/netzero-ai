## 🔹 Key Features

### ⚡ Energy Analytics
- Annual energy consumption (kWh)
- Energy cost analysis
- EPI (Energy Performance Index)
- CO₂ emissions

### ❄️ HVAC Engineering
- Cooling load (TR, kW)
- Sensible vs Latent heat analysis (SHR)
- Fresh air load & psychrometric calculations
- AHU airflow, duct sizing
- Chilled water flow & pump sizing
- Cooling tower sizing
- HVAC optimization potential

### 🌡️ Psychrometric Analysis
- Humidity ratio
- Enthalpy
- Dew point
- Fresh air load calculation

### ⚡ Electrical Design
- Transformer sizing
- DG sizing
- Cable & breaker sizing
- Voltage drop
- Power factor correction

### 💧 Water & Plumbing
- Water demand
- Underground & overhead tank sizing
- STP capacity
- Rainwater harvesting

### 🔥 Fire Fighting Design
- Fire tank sizing
- Fire pump sizing
- Jockey pump

### 🌞 Solar + Battery
- Solar capacity sizing (kWp)
- Roof utilization
- Battery backup sizing
- Net Zero balance

### 🌱 ESG & Sustainability
- ESG scoring
- CO₂ reduction tracking
- Waste & biogas estimation

### 📄 PDF Report
- Auto-generated professional engineering report
